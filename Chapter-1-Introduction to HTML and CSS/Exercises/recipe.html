Chicken Noodle Recipe

Noodles are made from unleavened dough that is rolled flat and cut stretched, or extruded into long strips or strings.Noodles can be kept refrigerated for short periods of time or dried and stored for later use.

|

It's time to prepare the noodles

  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients For the Recipe

  • Salt and Oil.
  • Cold water.
  • Carrots and garlic paste.
  • The Noodles.
  • Seasoning.

Instructions

1.

The Start: Bring water to a boil, add salt and 1 tsp oil. Now add in noodles and cook till done. Drain and rinse in cold water. Set aside.

2.

Process: Heat oil in a pan, add eggs, season with salt and pepper and scramble. Set aside. Now add chicken, add ginger garlic paste, salt and mix well.

3.

Add ons: Add in carrot, cabbage, onions, bell pepper, along with salt and sugar and toss well. Add noodles and mix well.

4.

Add ons: Now add in all sauces, vinegar and mix well.

5.

Last: Stir fry on high heat for 5 to 7 mins.Add pepper and spring onion green. Mix well.
